This Application will give us a idea of what kind of modification for Age of Empires 3 you are looking to make. We will tell you if we think a idea will be hard or impossible to implement. We will tailor tutorials to serve your needs as a modder starting out and of course be reachable by contact with any questions or concerns. Answer all questions and submit using the submit button when done.
