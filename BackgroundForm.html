Modding/Technical Background Form


People have many different starting points in their modding careers. Some are computer science majors who know how programming works. Others may have expertise in graphic design or cad software. Others are a blank slate that have yet to learn anything. This form is meant to give us a understanding of what technical background you have in aspects that may make modding easier for you. Letting us know of any minimal modding experience you may have for Age of Empires 3 is useful as well. Answer all questions to the best of your ability for better results.
