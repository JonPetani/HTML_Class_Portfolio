Welcome Age of Empires 3 Fans!!!

You are here for a reason. You have decided the original Age of Empires 3 experience isn't enough for you. You wish to learn to mod(modify the game) this famous game so that you can customize it to yours and your user's liking. He prepared to find all the information you will ever need pertaining to modding here from programming tutorials to guides on how to create, modify, and add artwork.

This website will provide you all the needed information and helpful links to software needed to make your own Age of Empires 3 mod. This website is solely dedicated to modding and will go into detail on every subject to the extent that newcomers will not struggle as much as previous modders have. Our information is provided by experts in modding who have done this kind of work many times with success.

We take a different approach to teaching Age of Empires 3 modding than other sites. Instead of leaving learners on their own to experiment(though doing this as well is advised if you like) we build your knowledge base up on basic concepts like building a new civilization or programming a new card or technology with its own png icon step by step giving you all the tools you need to explore aspects of modding undiscovered still.

What is our Purpose?

We have you fill out a form detailed what kind of a game experience you are looking for (new units, new buildings, new civilization, reworks or overhauls of existing content) and provide you specially tailored information to suit your needs. We will email you information with language and detail that reflects your ambitions and background. Thats what sets us apart from other sites.

A little about us

This website was started by individuals who want to bring the artform of Age of Empires 3 modding to everyone. The more mods that exist, the more we can not only create our own strategy games based on the Age of Empires 3 engine, but we indrectly add value to Age of Empires 3 itself honoring as the wonderful real-time strategy game it is.

While you may have viewed modding tutorials before, ours are targeted to those without a programming/computer science background.

The developers of this website have seen some fantastic fan made modifications of this game over the past 10-15 years. Even though the game was created back in 2005, the fanbase is still large allowing any creation of yours to reach a much wider audience than you may expect.

We have ourselves attempted modding on a few occasions. We found the road to a finished product bumpy and unpaved even for those with a software background. We often found ourselves having to ask experts who had little patience or care for those that did not pick up on the material right away. Of course you will need patience, creativity and originallity to make a successful Age of Empires 3 mod, but wouldn't it be nice if there was real teacher there to facilitate your learning?

Important Warning

While modding can be fun, there is always the chance you can delete some code in a file that prevents the game from running completely or in certain aspects such as the ai. If you follow our instructions fully, this will not happen. Still keep in mind that we are not responsible for damage you do to your game files. If something happens that you have no backup for, simply reinstall the game and it will work like it did before.
