Advice When Attending Performance

In General, you should try to get a reservation on these days as the restaurant gets quite packed when such a great band comes to perform. We recommend giving us 24-48 hours in advance since we may not find a table for you otherwise. When the band is playing, try not be a distration or you will be asked to leave (Especially don't try to get on stage or come near the band members). Don't mind the armed guards we hire at these events for both the safety of the performers and of our customers. If you can, invite people you know to be in one party, so we can seat you all at one table to avoid the loss of space for additional parties.

Interesting Facts about the Eagles

We have some facts about the Eagles here incase you have never heard of this band before.

  • The Eagles are a Rock Band.
  • The band was started in 1971
  • Has had some famous albums like Long Road Out of Eden, Hotel California, and Hell Freezes Over
  • The band originally consisted of Glenn Frey, Don Henley, Bernie Leadon, and Randy Meisner with Glenn and Don being the most signifigant members
  • More than 100 million albums were sold worldwide making them very popular not just in the U.S who has seen 120 million albums being sold
  • Songs cover a few special topics including the song Hole in the World which is tied to 9/11 and Long Road Out of Eden and I Dreamed There was No War which are related to the experiences of U.S veterens.
